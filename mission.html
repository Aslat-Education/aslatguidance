Mission

At ASLAT Guidance Education Consultancy, our mission is to redefine the study abroad experience for Nepali international students. We aim to provide practical, experience-based guidance that empowers students to achieve their academic and career goals without unnecessary hurdles. By offering personalized support—from selecting the right courses to planning life abroad—we strive to ensure every student feels confident, prepared, and supported throughout their journey.

Vision

Our vision is to build a future where Nepali students are globally recognized for their achievements, driven by informed decisions and strategic planning. We aspire to be the go-to consultancy in Nepal, setting a new standard of trust, transparency, and tailored services. At ASLAT, we envision creating a community of empowered learners who thrive academically and personally, becoming role models for future generations.
