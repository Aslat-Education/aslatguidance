Spring Semester

The Spring Intake held from January to April, is the second most common choice for international students. Although fewer programs are available compared to the fall intake, the spring intake still provides ample opportunities for students to pursue their academic goals. One of the main advantages of this intake is the relatively lower competition, which might increase the chances of admission. The application process can be quicker, with deadlines usually falling between July and October. While students joining in the spring may miss out on some major events and activities that begin in the fall, they still benefit from an immediate start in the new year and a potentially smoother admission process.
