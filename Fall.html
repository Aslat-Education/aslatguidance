Fall Semester

The Fall Intake, occurring between September and December, is the most popular and preferred intake among international students, including those from Nepal. This season marks the beginning of the academic year in the United States, providing a comprehensive array of courses and programs. Most universities offer the majority of their scholarships and financial aid during this period, making it an ideal time for students seeking financial support. The fall intake also offers better access to internships and on-campus job opportunities, which can be crucial for gaining practical experience. Additionally, new students find it easier to integrate into the university community, thanks to numerous orientation programs and activities designed to welcome them. However, the high popularity of the fall intake means more competition, and students need to prepare and submit their applications early, typically between December and March of the previous year.
