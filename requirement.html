  • ➣ IELTS (International English Language Testing System)

    Undergraduate Program:

    Most universities require a minimum IELTS score of 6.0 for undergraduate admissions. Some institutions may have higher requirements, especially for competitive programs.

    Graduate Program:

    For master's and doctoral programs, the minimum IELTS score is usually around 6.5. Some programs, particularly those in fields requiring strong communication skills, may require higher scores, such as 7.0 or 7.5.

  • ➣ PTE (Pearson Test of English)

    Undergraduate Program:

    Most universities require a minimum PTE score of 50. This score indicates a moderate level of English proficiency suitable for undergraduate coursework.

    Graduate Program:

    For master's and doctoral programs, the minimum PTE score is typically around 55. Some competitive programs may require higher scores, such as 60 or more, especially in fields that demand strong communication skills.

  • ➣ TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language)

    Undergraduate Program:

    Most universities require a minimum TOEFL iBT score of 70-80. This score reflects a moderate level of English proficiency suitable for undergraduate studies.

    Graduate Program:

    For master's and doctoral programs, the minimum TOEFL iBT score is typically around 80-90. More competitive programs or prestigious universities may require higher scores, such as 100 or more, especially in fields that require strong communication skills.

  • ➣ DET (Duolingo English Test)

    Undergraduate Program:

    Most universities require a minimum Duolingo score of 95-120. This score range indicates a good level of English proficiency suitable for undergraduate coursework.

    Graduate Program:

    For master's and doctoral programs, the minimum Duolingo score is typically around 100-120. Some competitive programs or prestigious universities may require higher scores, such as 120 or above.
