OUR
STORY

ASLAT Guidance Education Consultancy was founded from a deep understanding of the international student experience. We’ve lived, studied, and thrived in the U.S. for over 10+ years. Moving abroad to study is a life-changing decision—one that brings excitement and opportunities, but also challenges like cultural adjustment, managing living costs, and navigating unfamiliar academic systems.
Our journey began with a passion for helping Nepali students planning to study in the U.S. avoid the obstacles we once faced. From choosing the right university and understanding visa requirements to managing finances and settling into a new country, we’ve been there, and we know what works.
We also understand the importance of effective academic planning. In the U.S., strategically planning your courses can save both time and money. That’s why we offer Post-Departure Services to guide students in selecting the right courses, mapping degree plans, and even navigating transfers if needed. Our goal is to ensure every student stays on track toward achieving their academic and career goals, without unnecessary delays or expenses.
At ASLAT, we are committed to providing personalized, end-to-end support—from your first application to settling in and thriving abroad. With us, your journey becomes a well-guided and empowering experience.
