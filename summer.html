Summer Program

The Summer Program taking place between May and August, is the least common intake among international students. This period typically offers limited course offerings, often focusing on short-term and specialized programs such as internships, language training, or accelerated courses. The summer intake is ideal for students who missed the fall or spring deadlines or those looking to fast-track their studies or career goals. The less crowded nature of this intake means students may receive more personalized attention from faculty and staff. However, the limited program availability and smaller student community during the summer can be a drawback for some.
