Mr AD Lama

Chairman and the Managing Director

Mr. AD Lama is the founder of ASLAT Guidance Education Consultancy and a seasoned professional with extensive experience in the U.S. education system. By profession, he is a Software Developer and Entrepreneur, combining technical expertise with visionary leadership to guide students on their educational journeys.
Having completed his Associate Degree, Bachelor’s Degree, and Master’s Degree from Southern New Hampshire University, Mr. Lama has firsthand knowledge of the academic pathways and challenges international students face. His journey—from navigating university transfers to achieving higher education success—has equipped him with invaluable insights to support and mentor students effectively.
Driven by a passion for empowering Nepali students, Mr. Lama ensures they plan their studies in the U.S. with confidence and precision, avoiding unnecessary hurdles and expenses. Under his leadership, ASLAT provides comprehensive services, including university selection, visa guidance, post-departure support, and degree planning. With his expertise, students receive personalized, goal-oriented support, making their study abroad experience smooth, successful, and transformative.
